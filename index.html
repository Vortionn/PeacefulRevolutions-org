Our Mission

People United for Peaceful Revolution (PUPR) encourages and enables ordinary people to participate in the democratic process by removing systemic barriers to political engagement. Through civic education, grassroots candidate empowerment, and platforms like UnitedWeRise.org, we aim to make democratic representative government more democratic and more representative.

When more people run for office and more voters are informed, government becomes more inclusive, transparent, and accountable.

About PUPR

501(c)(3) Nonprofit

People United for Peaceful Revolution, Inc. is incorporated in New York as an approved 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization, dedicated to strengthening democracy through grassroots engagement.

Civic Education

We provide resources and education to help citizens understand the democratic process and how they can effectively participate in their government.

Candidate Empowerment

Through platforms like UnitedWeRise.org, we support grassroots candidates and help remove barriers to political participation.
